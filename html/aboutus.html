ARTICLES OF CREATING CRAFTS:

The process of producing handicrafts made by the traditional artists of the nation for a long time such as ceramic products, bamboo and rattan, lacquer mosaic ...

The first is to shape products with ceramic, bamboo or wood materials, these materials have been treated against warping, termite, cleaning and shaping products in many ways by the manual method:

If it is a lacquer product: The product is covered by curtain fabric and painted with a single layer of wool paint and many times sharpened (About 10 times) makes that layer of gloss beautiful according to the required color. This paint has been tested and allowed to be used safely and hygienically.

If bamboo products were soaked in water against termites for about 6 months. Then split into a product, smooth it. If it is white, it is a natural color and the color is due to painting on the product about 5 times. Use dry, do not store water. Safe for use in food, fruit.

If it is a product that touches eggshell and son shells: Ceramic or wood products, bamboo is touched on an eggshell or a boy's shell. Eggshell duck after hatching or male shell cleaned with alcohol and dried at high temperature. This product is durable, beautiful and safe to use.

These handicraft products have been tested and permitted by the State of Vietnam for production and use. In addition, when sterilizing countries, they must be disinfected according to each controlled shipment of Vietnam Customs.
